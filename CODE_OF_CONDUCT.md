# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Short version: be respectful, assume good faith, attack problems not people. Harassment, discrimination, and sustained disruption are not tolerated.

Reports: open a private security advisory or email the maintainers listed on the repository page. Maintainers will review and respond within a week, and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban accounts at their discretion.
